Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Noor Beauty Salon ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use our app:

  • Phone number — used for account creation and OTP-based login.
  • Name — used to personalise your profile and bookings.
  • Booking details — services booked, dates, and preferences.

How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To process and confirm your salon bookings.
  • To send booking confirmations and OTP codes via SMS.
  • To improve our services and app experience.

Data Security

All data is transmitted over HTTPS (encrypted in transit). We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, your personal data and booking history are permanently removed from our systems.

Account Deletion

You can delete your account at any time from the app's Profile section, or by visiting our Account Deletion page. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
