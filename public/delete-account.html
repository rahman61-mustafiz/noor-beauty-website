Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. To permanently delete your Noor Beauty Salon account and all associated data, follow the steps below inside the app.

How to delete your account

1
Open the Noor Beauty Salon app on your phone.
2
Tap the Profile icon at the bottom of the screen.
3
Scroll down and tap "Delete my account".
4
Select a reason and tap Confirm to permanently delete your account.
⚠️ This action is permanentDeleting your account will permanently remove your profile, booking history, and all personal data. This cannot be undone.
